Frequently Asked

What is your minimum order requirement?

Our minimum order varies by service type. For small chops, we require a minimum of 50 pieces. For full catering services, we recommend a minimum of 30 guests to ensure the best experience and value.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for standard events. For large-scale events, weddings, or destination services, please book 4-6 weeks ahead to ensure availability and optimal planning.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and other dietary requirements. Please inform us during booking so we can customize your menu accordingly.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Surulere, Lagos, and serve all of Lagos and Abuja. We also provide destination catering services internationally. Additional logistics fees may apply for locations outside Lagos mainland.

What is included in your service?

Our service includes menu consultation, food preparation, professional presentation, serving staff (for larger events), and cleanup. We also provide serving equipment, utensils, and disposables as needed.

What are your payment terms?

We require a 50% deposit upon booking confirmation, with the balance due 48 hours before your event. We accept bank transfers and mobile payments. Full payment terms will be outlined in your service agreement.
